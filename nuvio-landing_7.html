FAQ

Questions we
get a lot.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and we'll get back to you in under an hour.

Is there a free plan?
Yes! You can get started completely free — no credit card required. The free plan includes up to 5 active projects and one workspace, which is more than enough to feel the difference Nuvio makes.
Can I use Nuvio on mobile?
Absolutely. Nuvio is available as a native app on iOS and Android, and is fully responsive on any browser. Your data syncs instantly across every device you use.
How does Focus Mode work?
Focus Mode collapses everything except your current task. Notifications pause, the sidebar disappears, and a Pomodoro-style timer kicks in. It's the fastest way to get into deep work.
Can I cancel or switch plans anytime?
Yes, always. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your settings at any time. If you cancel a paid plan, you keep access until the end of your billing period — no surprises.
Is my data private and secure?
We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties, and you can export or delete your account entirely at any time.
Does Nuvio integrate with other tools?
Yes. Nuvio connects with Slack, Google Calendar, Notion, Linear, and more via our integrations hub. An open API is also available on the Pro and Team plans.
Still have questions?
Our support team typically responds in under an hour, Monday through Friday.
